    Project Overview

    Luna Essence is a minimalistic, elegant and luxurious fragrance shop, which enhances the feeling of empowerment and self discovery.

    Problem Statement

    The majority of participants were dissatisfied due to overwhelming selection of a products, inadequate product descriptions, unavailable free fragrance samples, and no option for creating their own perfumes with limited knowledge.

    User Interviews

    In the pursuit of shaping the app's design, I conducted user interviews aiming to glean valuable insights that could significantly impact the decision-making process.

    Questions

    • How would you describe your current experience when searching for a right perfume?
    • What aspects do you find most challenging?
    • In your ideal online fragrance shopping experience, what type of information and features would you consider essential, and why?
    • Would you be interested in creating your own perfume with ease, and why?
    • What motivates you to buy a fragrance online, and why?

    Based on the findings from the interview session, I will prioritise following key features:

    • 1.Streamlined product selection
    • 2. Enhanced product descriptions
    • 3. Free fragrance sample option
    • 4. Easy to follow perfume creation

    User Stories

    • “As a customer, I want to be able to place multiple items in a shopping cart, so that I can purchase more then one item at a time”
    • “As a frequent customer, I want to see recommendations of products I may like, so that I can find items I might not otherwise have on my own”
    • “As a returning customer, I want to be able to save items that I cannot buy to a wish list so that I can purchase them at a later date”

    Brand Guidelines

    My Mission

    At Luna Essence, my mission is to redefine the way individuals experience and connect with the world of perfumes.

    I envision a fragrance journey that transcends the ordinary, blending the realms of freshness, modernity, and exclusivity.

    Logo

    The Moon, a symbol of potent energy and emotional ebb and flow, mirrors Luna Essence's spectrum of feelings.

    Just as moonlight reveals the unseen, our brand illuminates the path to individuality, luxury, and self-discovery through the art of fragrance.

    Colour Scheme

    • The primary use of dark black exudes a sense of sophistication and timelessness, anchoring Luna Essence in a realm of enduring elegance.
    • Tile, a contemporary and trendy hue aligning with the current fashion trends.
    • The choice of white for text brings balance, ensuring clarity and readability, while collectively, these colours create a visually striking and luxurious palette that resonates with modern aesthetics and timeless appeal.
